Spare Parts

Mahindra Powerol has a strong network of 138 service dealers called MAGEICs (Mahindra Authorised Gensets & Industrial Engines Centers), spread all over the country. Supplies of Spare parts to all our valued customers are catered through these MAGIECs network. All our MAGIECs are well trained to maintain adequate stocks of spare parts, based on the MAHINDRA Inventory Management system. The inventory levels of all the MAGIECS are regularly monitored & reveiewed by our CCMs and HO, to ensure total customer satisfaction We have a well established & state of the art Central Spare warehouse at Wagholi, Pune where sufficient is maintained and monitored to cater the spares requirement to all the MAGIECS. Well defined systems are in place at this central depot, to meet the most stringent expectations of the customers. Also, for some other crucial parts we have direct arrangement in place wherein spare parts are supplied directly from vendors to our MAGIECS.
